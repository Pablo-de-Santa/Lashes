LASHES

Classic eyelash extensions are – as their namesake – classic. They are simple, beautiful, natural-looking lash extensions. They are applied on a 1:1 ratio, which means one extension on one natural lash. This allows you to achieve an understated, natural enhancement.

Classic lashes

Classic Lashing is a technique in which a single extension is applied to one, isolated natural lash. Classic lashes are perfect for adding length and some fullness to your natural lash line.

2-D Lashes

A lash thinner than Classic but thicker than volume. Also adding definition and fullness. Two synthetic lases can be placed on one natural lash creating a two demential effect.

LASHES

The lashes will still lay flat on your eyes, but their equipped definition causes them to flip out a bit more creating a bouncier, fuller looking lash. 3D lashes also hold a curl much better, allowing you to better shape your lashes and keeping them intact for longer.

3-D Lashes

3D lashes have a layered effect. They are handmade by combining different sizes of lashes, mixing short and long hairs giving the lashes a more voluminous, feathery look. This style overall tends to be more glamorous and elegant. If you’re searching for more noticeable lashes, 3D lashes are your best bet to achieve ultimate drama.
